COE: Taxes on Private Car Ownership

The Certificate of Entitlement (COE) grants the legal right of the holder to register, own and use a vehicle in Singapore for a period of 10 years. The price of a COE, however, often exceeds the value of the car itself. See the trends in COE quotas and prices since 2002.

Mapping Dengue Clusters

Dengue is endemic to Southeast Asia and Singapore is no exception. More than 40,000 cases of dengue fever were reported in the 2013-2014 epidemic. View past and present dengue clusters on an interactive map and timeline.
